about WeatherX

Using Geocoding, WeatherX gets current and forecasted weather for any location on Earth

You simply enter a Zip or Province Code, City, County, State or Country.

You will not promopted to allow use of your location, since no GeoLocaton is done

You can track current and 7-day forecast on multiple locations

A simple and elegant app.
